Victor's areas of interest:

Jersey City seems to get a bad rap, but it's cool, chill, and really underrated. Much better than "Broboken" that's next to us.

Wanna try out some places in Jersey City? Here are some of the places to check out

Barcade

Retro gaming and alcohol goes hand in hand! Sister location to the Barcade in Brooklyn, it's a great place to go play retro aracade games, drink craft beer, and sneer at the hipsters, while you totally aren't one at all.

Zeppelin Hall

German styled beer hall, with huge interior, good food, and lots of German beer. Don't try to come here to only have one or two drinks. That's impossible.

Thirty Acres

Great fushion foodie styled restaurant. Even has some press in NYC! The menu changes very quickly, so check out their tumblr to get up to date dishes.
